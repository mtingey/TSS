THE BAND

The Signal Sound is an alternative rock band from Salt Lake City who are doing it for their love of the music. Drawing primary influences from bands of the 90s and the Aughts, The Signal Sound creates their brand of modern alternative rock with the aim of enjoying the music they love together with others.

With albums available on all major digital distribution channels, SLUG magazine had this to say of The Signal Sound's most recently released album, Broken Homes: "there are heaps of visual imagery...The lyrics are evocative; the smart syncopation indicates that the band’s percussionist is an apparent pro; the vocals are clean; and the execution of their genre feels expert."

The Signal Sound would love to share music with you! Find copies of our recordings on all major digital music distribution outlets and come have a great time with us at our next show!
